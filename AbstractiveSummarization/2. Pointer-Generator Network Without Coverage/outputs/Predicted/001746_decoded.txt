billy joe saunders and chris eubank jnr could rematch in under six weeks after the pair reignited their war of words at a london press conference on thursday .
the pair clashed in a memorable 12-round grudge match on november 29 with british , commonwealth and european middleweight champions saunders handing his rival his first career defeat .
now the duo are both set to fight at wembley arena on may 9 but not against each other .
