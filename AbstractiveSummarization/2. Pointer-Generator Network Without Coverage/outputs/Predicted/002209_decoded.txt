nikola karabatic will stand trial for fraud over his suspected involvement in an illegal betting case .
karabatic , a multiple world and olympic gold medalist , and his younger brother luka were among a group of players banned by the french league two years ago for betting on the result of a match in which their montpellier club lost to a struggling team .
karabatic has denied placing bets on the match that montpellier lost 31-28 to cesson-rennes .
