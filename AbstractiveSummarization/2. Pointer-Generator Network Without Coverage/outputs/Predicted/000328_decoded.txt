daisy bentley has scoured the streets of cities and towns looking for those odd bits of paper that flutter to the ground when their owners are n't looking .
her quest has uncovered a fascinating , if idiosyncratic , collection of shopping lists , reminders , requests , love notes and motivational musings , all poignant pieces of others ' lives .
bentley feels it is part of human nature to hold on to items , no matter how small or insignificant they may seem .
