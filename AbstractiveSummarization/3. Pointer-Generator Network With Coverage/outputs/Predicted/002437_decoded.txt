liverpool and newcastle players took part in a minute silence before their premier league match at anfield on monday night in memory of the 96 fans who died at hillsborough almost 26 years ago .
the fixture at anfield , which took place two days before the 26th anniversary of the disaster , was preceded by a period of silence from both teams and the 45,000 fans gathered .
flowers were also laid at the club 's hillsborough memorial at anfield , including some by former newcastle captain bobby moncur .
