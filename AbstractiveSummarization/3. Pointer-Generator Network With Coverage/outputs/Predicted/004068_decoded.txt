zlatan ibrahimovic and dimitri payet have had their respective suspensions for abusing ligue 1 referees reduced by one match apiece .
paris saint-germain striker ibrahimovic will now miss three games and marseille playmaker payet just one after both clubs succeeded with appeals to the french olympic committee -lrb- cnosf -rrb- .
ibrahimovic was initially banned for four games by the french olympic committee -lrb- cnosf -rrb- .
